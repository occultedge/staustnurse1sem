Nursing 1st Semester - Course Modules

1. Medical Sociology and Anthropology (15 hours)

Introduction to Medical Sociology (5 hours)
Social Determinants of Health (5 hours)
Cultural Anthropology in Healthcare (5 hours)

2. General Biochemistry (20 hours)

Introduction to Biochemistry (5 hours)
Biomolecules: Structure and Function (5 hours)
Metabolism and Enzymatic Reactions (5 hours)
Clinical Biochemistry Applications (5 hours)

3. General Microbiology and Parasitology (25 hours)

Introduction to Microbiology (5 hours)
Bacteria, Viruses, Fungi (10 hours)
Introduction to Parasitology (5 hours)
Microbial Pathogenesis and Immunity (5 hours)

4. General Pharmacology I (20 hours)

Principles of Pharmacology (5 hours)
Pharmacokinetics and Pharmacodynamics (5 hours)
Drug Classifications and Mechanisms (5 hours)
Adverse Effects and Drug Interactions (5 hours)

5. Anatomy and Physiology IA (20 hours)

Introduction to Anatomy and Physiology (5 hours)
The Skeletal and Muscular Systems (5 hours)
Nervous System and Special Senses (5 hours)
Cardiovascular System (5 hours)

6. Health Care Ethics (15 hours)

Introduction to Health Care Ethics (5 hours)
Ethical Dilemmas in Nursing (5 hours)
Professional Ethics and Responsibilities (5 hours)

7. Medico Sanitary Techniques (15 hours)

Sanitation and Hygiene Practices (5 hours)
Basic Infection Control (5 hours)
Sterilization and Disinfection Techniques (5 hours)

8. Medical Law and Citizenship (15 hours)

Medical Law and Patient Rights (5 hours)
Citizenship and Health Care (5 hours)
Legal Implications in Nursing (5 hours)

9. Anatomy and Physiology IB (20 hours)

Respiratory System (5 hours)
Digestive System (5 hours)
Urinary System (5 hours)
Endocrine and Reproductive Systems (5 hours)

10. ICT I (15 hours)

Computer course for nurses(12 Hrs)
Using Healthcare Information Systems (5 hours)
Electronic Health Records and Data Security (5 hours)

11. Functional English (15 hours)

Basic Grammar and Vocabulary (5 hours)
Medical Terminology in English (5 hours)
Communication Skills in Healthcare (5 hours)
The course includes learning videos, live online lectures by eminent mentors from India (2 hours per week), books, quizzes, assignments, and practical work. At the end of the course, students have the option of a 1-month internship in India. Certificates and degrees are awarded upon completion.
